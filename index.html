TRAINING QUICK GUIDE

Handling duplicate profiles

  1. Search Admin for all user-related accounts.
  2. Confirm no account is permanently deactivated by Trust & Safety before changing information.
  3. Determine the primary account by comparing consumer report status, identity details, completed Gigs and account activity.
  4. Keep duplicate accounts locked. Unlock only the confirmed primary account.
  5. Remove driver’s license information from each duplicate profile.
  6. Add a note to the duplicate profile identifying the primary account, and a note to the primary profile identifying the duplicates.
